UI/UX Design

The UX designer role is to make a product or service usable, enjoyable, and accessible. While many companies design user experiences, the term is most often associated with digital design for websites and apps. While the exact process varies from product to product and company to company, the general phases of design tend to stay the same.

App Design

App design is the look and feel of a mobile application. This includes all of the visual elements and interactive elements that impact how the app functions. Mobile app design combines two concepts—UI and UX. The user interface (UI) encompasses the app’s look and feel. It includes color, font, app style, and similar design choices. The user experience (UX) design prioritizes the app’s functions and use.
